The evolution of an icon

To evolve means to improve to be able to answer to new challenges. In order to accommodate the future and be a part of it, TIM is also renewed in the brand. Since its birth, more than 20 years ago, its logo was a sign of modernity and dynamism: a promise of projects and new horizons. Today icontinues to be a symbol of change, combining the expertise of two realities: the red trigram of Telecom Italia, the intense blue of the TIM brand and a T to unite these two worlds. The synthesis of a great legacy and a strong orientation to the future. The result is a simple and instant icon: evolved, for evolved times.

Key values of TIM

We are responsible leaders

Being a leader means having a vision that goes beyond the horizons of knowledge, lead by example, be reference models. We believe in the culture of inclusion, considering confrontation an opportunity and we promise to encourage dialogue.

We wish to grow

Always keep the passion alive, feeding curiosity with the firm desire to improve is one of our primary concerns: we remain open to the world and forward to the future.


We aim for excellence

Commitment and dedication are essential elements to develop the highest quality projects, made with a constant eye to innovation and to the needs of our customers.

We live the client

We work for making people communicate, making their lives easier, rich in opportunities and relationships. All this through a way of caring, development and mutual enrichment that meets the needs, the aspirations and dreams of our customers.
