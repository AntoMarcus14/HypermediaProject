The evolution of an icon

To evolve means to improve to be able to answer to new challenges. In order to accommodate the future and be a part of it, TIM is also renewed in the brand. Since its birth, more than 20 years ago, its logo was a sign of modernity and dynamism: a promise of projects and new horizons. Today icontinues to be a symbol of change, combining the expertise of two realities: the red trigram of Telecom Italia, the intense blue of the TIM brand and a T to unite these two worlds. The synthesis of a great legacy and a strong orientation to the future. The result is a simple and instant icon: evolved, for evolved times.

Testimonials

In recent years, the way we communicate has dramatically changed. Maybe you can not even talk about communication, new technologies in fact are leading us into an era where the connections between individuals are more profound and meaningful.
TIM, which has always been synonymous of communication, has radically transformed to become a company whose technology allows people to connect with each other and communicate. TIM launch campaign of the new positioning was born to tell this new connected world, through the involvement of three exceptional testimonials that present their point of view:
Sir Tim Berners-Lee,Fabio Fazio and Pif.

Tim Berners-Lee

Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee is a British computer scientist, inventor of the World Wide Web and promoter of the Internet as a communication tool to grow both from the cultural and economic point of view.
No one better than him, you can tell how the hyper-connectivity is redefining the way we live, increasing our chances and evolving the way we relate to others and the world around us by supporting the new campaign concept.

Fabio Fazio

Fabio Fazio is one of the leading figures of Italian television. It enjoys great popularity and his style is ironic and biting.
Intellectual and opinion leader, in his career he has had the chance to interview many leading figures: not only television celebrities homegrown and international, but also, and especially, scholars, researchers and scientists, politicians, writers, musicians, Nobel prizes. Always polite, never over the top, but witty and sharp in every speech, never misses an opportunity to demonstrate a strong cultural curiosity, which leaves emerge its nature investigator of reality and the world around.

Pif

Pif is one of the most interesting figures and details of the Italian scene. He owes much of its fame to its own unique style always spontaneous and his ability to speak bluntly to each subject, even of the most burning. His great critical spirit made him in the public eye a sort of witness and commentator of the country and its changes.
